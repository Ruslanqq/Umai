Our Service

Residential Moves

We specialize in residential moves, helping individuals and families relocate their homes with ease. Our team of professionals will handle everything from packing and loading to transportation and unloading, ensuring a smooth and stress-free move to your new home.

Commercial Moves

Umai Moving Solutions is experienced in commercial relocations, catering to businesses of all sizes. Whether you're moving offices, warehouses, or retail spaces, we have the expertise to handle all aspects of your commercial move efficiently, minimizing downtime and ensuring a seamless transition.

Local Moves

If you're moving within the same city or nearby area, our local moving services are designed to meet your specific needs. Our local movers are familiar with the area and will ensure a swift and efficient move, making your transition to a new location hassle-free.

Long-Distance Moves

Moving across the country? Umai Moving Solutions offers long-distance moving services to help you relocate to any destination within the United States. With our extensive network of partners and affiliates, we can coordinate all the logistics of your long-distance move, providing a reliable and efficient service.

Packing and Unpacking

Save time and effort with our professional packing and unpacking services. Our skilled packers will carefully pack your belongings using high-quality packing materials, ensuring their safety during transit. Upon arrival at your new location, we can also assist with unpacking and setting up your items, helping you settle in quickly.

Storage Solutions

If you require temporary storage for your belongings, we provide secure storage solutions. Our storage facilities are equipped with advanced security systems to ensure the safety of your items. Whether you need short-term or long-term storage, we can accommodate your specific requirements.

Specialty Item Handling

We understand that certain items require special care during a move. Whether you have valuable artwork, antiques, pianos, or other delicate items, our experienced movers have the expertise to handle them safely and ensure their protection throughout the moving process.

Furniture Assembly and Disassembly

Our team can assist with disassembling and reassembling furniture as needed during the moving process. This service ensures that your large furniture pieces are properly handled and fit through doorways and tight spaces.
